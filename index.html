About

I received a B.S. in Computer Science and Technology from Renmin University of China in 2024 and an M.S. in Data Science from Fudan University in 2026. During my master's studies, I spent one year (2025-2026) working at a United Nations agency in Africa under sponsorship from the China Scholarship Council (CSC).

My research interests lie in AI Agents, Multi-Agent Systems, agent coordination, and agentic workflows. I am broadly interested in building intelligent systems capable of effective collaboration, communication, and decision-making in complex environments. Please feel free to contact me.
